It's Ski Time

Ivo Salmre is a recent addition to the Tableau team. He came to Seattle most recently from the city of Vancouver, B.C., and he's a ski nut, like many of us at Tableau.

Ivo created this visualization of all the ski areas in North America. It's great for comparing vertical drop, annual snowfall and more. While it's a great resource for any skier, I suspect he did because he left his skis up at Whistler and was trying to convince himself it was well worth the trip to get them.

Ellie Fields
Senior Vice President of Product Marketing at Tableau
December 10, 2010
